Fapr Privacy Policy

Fapr will collect certain bits of information that all users provide. When signing up for Tier 2 & 3 memberships, Fapr will ask the user to provide front of government issued identification with picture (driver's license, passport, or ID card). We only collect this information as a one time event (unless another profile is created). We will not ask for an updated ID in future. We collect this information only for storage unless illegal activities are detected and/or reported.

We also collect this information to provide a safe environment for all parties. This is an adult only site and Fapr will do its very best to protect children. We do not want children to misuse this site nor do we want adults to mistakenly talk to children and end up in trouble with authorities. The collecting of this information is also needed to minimize, and possibly eliminate, spammers, fake profiles and robots.When a user fills out their profile, the information provided will be stored for display only with the users agreement of the Terms of Service.

Fapr will also collect and store credit/debit card information for billing purposes only. There will never be any charges unless initiated and authorized by user.

Fapr will NEVER sell or provide user information to a third party (unless illegal activity). Fapr reserves the right to use user's preferences, posts, and content to display additional content (ads and suggestions) through algorithms. For any questions, please feel free to reach out support.
