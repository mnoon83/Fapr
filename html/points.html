Fapr points

While enjoying the fuits of the pleasure that is Fapr, you'll also be able to earn points.

There is a variety of ways to earn points and spend them too.

Earning

First, you'll earn 100 points simply for signing up.

There will be an additional 100 points for completing your Tier 1 profile. After signing up, you'll earn 10 points for your posts (up to 5 posts per day) and every interaction with other profiles (up to 25 interations per day). There will be referral 50 points when someone signs up using your referal code.

For every dollar spend on Tier membership, you'll earn 1 point.

On top of daily 10 points, there will be a free daily spin for additional points (ranging 10-500 points).

You can earn 1 point per dollar spent in the merch store. (when it opens).

We will have a monthly sweepstakes for larger amount of points. The amount of points won will depend on number of entries. Winner will be award full amount of entries.

There will also be random giveaways and surprises of points and merch.


Spending

You are welcome to save up or spend as many points as you want. When you do decide to spend your points, you can spend them on Tier membership monthly cost, merchandise, and sweepstakes entries.

Tier membership will cost can be applied starting at 1000 points

Merchandise will have point cost displayed on each individual item

Sweepstakes enteries will be 1 point per entry.


ALL points will have no expiration date and no maximum.


Levels and Badges

All user points earned will be accumulated to earn badges. Badge level progress will displayed on user account and not visible to others. Points and badge will be shown on profile. The badge will be displayed next to user avatar.

Spending points will NOT decrease level progress.
