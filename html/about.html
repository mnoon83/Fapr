About Fapr

Fapr is a place to share adult content with others. Not all content will have nudity but dont be shy when it shows up. It is meant to be a free and peaceful place to express desires. Whether those desires are hidden or full out in the open, Fapr is the place to share it all. No matter what you like, we are here to help you show it off. This is meant for sexully explicit content but also anything is welcome, even cat memes. Please feel free to share anything you want.

Fapr is the idea that something had been taken away from people when a certain other app took away something vital for many people. We wanted to bring that part of the internet back into the hands of the people. Fapr is meant for adult content of any sort that is out there. Gay, straight, trans, furry, leather, BDSM, or whatever else you are into. There is nothing taboo about being on Fapr and liking what you like. Please feel free to share whatever you want. Even if it's totally vanilla, we are happy that you decided to join our family and share it out loud.

Aside from the posts that are shared, Fapr wants to bring communities together. We know that the other site took away a lot of connections and we want to help bring those connections back. There were plenty of colorful personalities out there and we want to help everyone feel welcome. This is an open platform to be who you want to be. There is no room on Fapr for hate or shaming. If you dont like something, keep scrolling or use the elipses (...) to indicate you're not interested.

As we move forward, there will be bigger and better changes. Thank you from the bottom of our hearts that you have decided to become part of our Fapr community.
